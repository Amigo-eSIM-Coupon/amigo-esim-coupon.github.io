How to Activate Amigo eSIM on iPhone & Android

Complete eSIM installation guide for all compatible devices. Takes under 2 minutes from purchase to connected.

Updated April 2026  ·  5 min read  ·  Works for iPhone XS+ and Android eSIM devices

Before You Begin

Make sure your device supports eSIM. Here's a quick compatibility check:

  • iPhone: iPhone XS, XR, and all newer models support eSIM
  • Samsung: Galaxy S20 and newer, Z Fold/Flip series
  • Google Pixel: Pixel 3 and newer
  • Other Android: Check Settings → About Phone → SIM status for eSIM support

You'll also need a WiFi connection during setup and your QR code from Amigo eSIM (sent by email within 30 seconds of purchase).

Activate on iPhone – Step by Step

1
Open Settings

Go to Settings on your iPhone. Tap Cellular (or Mobile Data depending on your region).

2
Add eSIM

Tap Add Cellular Plan or Add eSIM. Your camera will open automatically.

3
Scan the QR Code

Point your camera at the QR code in your Amigo eSIM confirmation email. The code will be detected automatically.

4
Confirm Installation

Tap Continue on the confirmation screen. Your eSIM profile will download and install (takes ~30 seconds).

5
Set as Travel Line

Choose a label for your new eSIM (e.g., "Travel" or "Amigo"). Set it as your default data line or keep your home SIM as default and switch manually when abroad.

6
Enable Data Roaming

On your Amigo eSIM line, enable Data Roaming. This is required for the eSIM to connect to local networks in your destination country.

Activate on Android – Step by Step

1
Open Settings

Go to Settings → Network & Internet → SIMs (path may vary by Android version).

2
Add SIM / Download eSIM

Tap the + icon or Add SIM. Select Scan QR code or Download a SIM instead.

3
Scan the QR Code

Scan the QR code from your Amigo eSIM confirmation email. Alternatively, tap the manual entry option and enter the activation code manually.

4
Confirm and Download

Confirm the eSIM download when prompted. The profile will install automatically within 30–60 seconds.

5
Set as Data SIM

Set your Amigo eSIM as the preferred data SIM in Settings. Enable data roaming on the Amigo eSIM line.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

QR code won't scan
Ensure you have good lighting and hold your camera steady. Try zooming in slightly. If it still fails, use the manual activation code option.
eSIM not connecting abroad
Make sure Data Roaming is enabled on the Amigo eSIM line. Toggle airplane mode on/off to force network reconnection.
"No service" after activation
The eSIM activates on arrival in the destination country. If you're still at home, it may show no service until you travel.
QR code already used error
Each QR code can only be scanned once. If you reset your phone, contact Amigo eSIM support for a replacement code.
Can I use two eSIMs at once?
Depends on your device. iPhone 13+ supports dual eSIM (two active eSIMs simultaneously). Most Android devices support one active eSIM at a time.
How do I switch between SIMs?
On iPhone: Settings → Cellular. On Android: Settings → Network → SIM management. You can switch your active data SIM instantly.
