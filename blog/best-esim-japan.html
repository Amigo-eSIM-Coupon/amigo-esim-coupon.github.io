Japan eSIM Tips & Tricks

  • Download your eSIM profile before you land — airport WiFi can be slow and crowded
  • Japan is 4G LTE excellent but 5G is city-centre only — don't pay a 5G premium if visiting rural areas
  • Enable Low Data Mode on iPhone or Data Saver on Android to stretch your GB
  • Pocket WiFi devices are popular in Japan but eSIM is more convenient and usually cheaper
