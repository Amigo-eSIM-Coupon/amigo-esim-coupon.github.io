US Coverage — What to Expect

Major US cities (New York, Los Angeles, Chicago, Miami, San Francisco) have excellent 4G LTE and 5G coverage. Rural and national park areas can have limited coverage — plan accordingly if you're hiking in places like Yellowstone or the Grand Canyon.

How Much Data for a USA Trip?

A 7-day US trip with navigation, social media, and some streaming typically requires 5–10GB. Business travelers relying on video calls may need 15GB or more. Use our data calculator guide for a personalized estimate.
